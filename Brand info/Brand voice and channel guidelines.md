## 4. Do's & Don'ts

### ‚úÖ Do's (On-Brand):
- Use proof points: "1M+ KL water saved, ROI in 2.3 years."
- Blend science + storytelling: data + human impact.
- Be transparent: show challenges and outcomes.
- Use inclusive, hopeful language: "together, we regenerate ecosystems."

### ‚ùå Don'ts (Off-Brand):
- Don't use vague claims: avoid "best solutions" or "world-class" without proof.
- Don't be overly technical: avoid jargon-heavy engineering terms for public channels.
- Don't fear-monger: avoid crisis-only messaging ("India will run out of water").
- Don't greenwash: never exaggerate impact or promise unrealistic outcomes.
