Optimization Strategy:
- Keyword focus: ESG consulting, water sustainability, corporate social responsibility, impact analytics.
- Tone: Professional, informative and solution-oriented for B2B decision-makers.
- Use clear H1 headings reflecting the primary intent of each page and supporting H2 subtopics covering benefits, how it works, regulations and strategic implications.
- Include FAQs with concise, helpful answers reflecting common user queries and real search intent; leverage structured FAQ schema markup for SEO.
- Prioritize helpful, human-like language that aligns with Google’s Helpful Content and E‑E‑A‑T principles.
- Add internal links between service pages to guide users and search engines through the site hierarchy.
- Use meta descriptions under 160 characters containing key terms and a compelling value proposition.

Observations & Assumptions:
- The sitemap is B2B-focused with no location-based pages.
- Service offerings include water sustainability (rainwater harvesting), ESG consulting, CSR strategy and impact analytics.
- Example FAQs and subtopics were crafted using publicly available information and typical search intent; local language variations can be added based on target audience research.
- A separate resources or blog section can be added later to target long-tail keywords and educate users.
